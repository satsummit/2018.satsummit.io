About

SatSummit convenes leaders in the satellite industry and experts in global development for in-depth conversations on solving the world's most critical development challenges with satellite data. From climate change to population growth to natural resource availability, earth observation data offers insights into today's biggest global issues.

SatSummit is committed to exploring the current state of satellite data capabilities and new systems. Our second SatSummit explored the topics ranging from machine learning to automated monitoring to identifying specific opportunities for partnership and innovation. Read more about the presentations, program launches and commitments, and news related to SatSummit on our Insights blog, and stay tuned for more information on SatSummit 3!

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is SatSummit?

    SatSummit is a two-day conference that convenes leaders in the satellite industry and experts in global development for in-depth conversations on solving the world's most critical development challenges with satellite data. From climate change to population growth to natural resource availability, Earth observation data offers insights into today's biggest global issues.

  2. How do I register?

    You can register here. Onsite registration will not be available.

  3. Am I able to purchase tickets for only one day of the event?

    Yes, we have ticket levels that include access to both days of SatSummit. You can also purchase tickets to attend Day 1 or Day 2 only.

  4. Will you make special accommodations for attendees observing Yom Kippur?

    Please reach out to us directly at info@satsummit.io to discuss dietary needs, schedule, and other accommodations that we can make while you're at SatSummit. If you are unable to attend Day 1 of SatSummit, we have separate tickets available for the Day 1 party at the Smithsonian National Air and Space Museum and for Day 2 of the summit. Please send your ticket request to info@satsummit.io.

  5. Where is SatSummit being held?

    SatSummit will take place at the Ronald Reagan Building and International Trade Center located at 1300 Pennsylvania Ave NW, Washington, DC 20004.

  6. Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

    The venue is fully ADA compliant. Should you need any accommodations to make this event accessible to you, please reach out to us at info@satsummit.io

  7. Is there a Mother’s room available if I am nursing?

    Yes, we have a private and comfortable space available for nursing Mother’s.
